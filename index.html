Our Story

Family Owned &
Ocean Fresh

After decades of expertise in the wholesale seafood business, Joe Willy's became our dream. We opened our doors in 2008 at Westside Plaza in Wappingers Falls and have been a fixture in this community ever since — thanks entirely to your continued support.

We source the freshest catch daily. From our signature New England Lobster Roll to our fresh-shucked raw bar, every plate is made with the same care that has kept families coming back for over 17 years.
