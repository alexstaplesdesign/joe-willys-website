# Getting Your Website Live — Joe Willy's Seafood House

Follow these steps in order to activate your new website.

---

## Step 1 — Create a Free Netlify Account

Netlify is the service that will host your website.

1. Go to **[netlify.com](https://netlify.com)**
2. Click **"Sign up"**
3. Sign up with your email address or Google account
4. Verify your email if prompted

That's all you need to do for now — you do not need to set anything else up inside Netlify yet.

---

## Step 2 — Notify Pixel Perfect Designs

Once your Netlify account is created, send your account email address to Pixel Perfect Designs so they can initiate the site transfer to your account.

---

## Step 3 — Accept the Transfer

1. You will receive an email from Netlify with the subject **"You've been invited to transfer a site"**
2. Open the email and click **"Accept transfer"**
3. Your website will now appear in your Netlify dashboard

> Your site is now live and under your ownership.

---

## Step 4 — Connect Your Domain *(if you have one)*

If you have a domain name (e.g. `joewillysseafoodhouse.com`), follow these steps:

1. Log into your Netlify dashboard and click on your site
2. Go to **Domain management → Add a domain**
3. Enter your domain name and click **Verify**
4. Netlify will provide you with DNS records — log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) and add them
5. Once updated, go to **Domain management → HTTPS** and click **"Provision certificate"** — this enables the secure `https://` padlock at no cost

> DNS changes can take a few hours to fully take effect.

---
